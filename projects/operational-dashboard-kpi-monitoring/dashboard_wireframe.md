# Dashboard Wireframe

## Layout Structure

### Top (KPI Cards)
- Triage Time
- Delivery Time
- Triage Delay
- Delivery Delay
- Completion Rate

### Left (Time Analysis)
- Line: Triage Time by Hour
- Column: Delay Rate by Hour

### Center (Operational Pressure)
- Scatter: Prescriptions vs Triage Time
- Column: Triage Time by Shift

### Right (Department Performance)
- Bar: Delay Rate by Department
- Bar: Delivery Delay by Department

### Bottom (Trend)
- Line: Monthly Volume

## Design Principles

- Show problems immediately
- Use color to highlight SLA breaches
- Avoid clutter
- Focus on decision-making, not decoration
